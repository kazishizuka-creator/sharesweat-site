ブランド戦略Brand Strategy

①②共通の憧れ「実直×洗練」を一本で張る。層ごとに変えるのは、見せ方・文脈・キャストだけ。Stand on one aspiration for ① and ②: "earnest × refined." What changes by layer is only the framing, context, and casting.

振りすぎると、両方を失うLean too far, and you lose both

①向け（ゆるく可愛く）に振りすぎると②が離れ、しかも①にも刺さらない。人は「なりたい自分」を買うからだ。逆に③向け（ガチ／計測／ストイック）に振りすぎると①②が引く（Runmetrixの轍）。Lean too far toward ① (loose and cute) and ② leaves — and it still won't land with ①, because people buy "the self they want to become." Lean too far toward ③ (hardcore / metrics / ascetic) and ① and ② recoil (the Runmetrix rut).

正解は、①②に共通する憧れ「実直×洗練（走りを生活の質として丁寧に高める人）」一本でブランドを張ること。DONUTという名前の柔らかさ（入口の低さ）とデザインの洗練（憧れの担保）が、それを両立させる装置になる。The answer is to stand the brand on the aspiration shared by ① and ②: "earnest × refined" — someone who carefully raises running as a quality of life. The softness of the name DONUT (a low entrance) and the refinement of the design (the guarantee of aspiration) are the device that holds both together.

ペルソナThe persona

34歳・都内専門職34, a Tokyo professional

朝6時半、駒沢か皇居を10km。走りが綺麗。On のシューズにミニマルなウェア。サブ4を余裕で走るが記録を自慢しない。10年間無故障。データは見るが、追われない。①はこの人の「生活」に憧れ、②は「走り」に憧れる — どちらにとっても半歩先。参照人物像: 三津家貴也／知性側に 為末大。6:30 a.m., a 10 km loop at Komazawa or the Imperial Palace. Runs beautifully. On shoes, minimal apparel. Clears sub-4 with ease but never boasts about times. Ten years injury-free. Reads the data but isn't chased by it. ① aspires to this person's life; ② aspires to their running — half a step ahead for both. References: Mitsuka Takaya; on the intellect side, Dai Tamesue.
